## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**  
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup required to see your audit results. 
We ask for your email after we show you the findings — never before.

**Q: How do you know the pricing data is accurate?**  
A: We manually verify every price against official vendor pricing pages 
each week. Sources are cited in our open pricing registry.

**Q: Do you store my data?**  
A: Your tool names and spend figures are stored to generate your 
shareable report URL. No payment info, no API keys, no sensitive data.

**Q: What is Credex?**  
A: Credex sources discounted AI credits from companies that overforecast 
their usage. High-savings audits may receive an optional intro to Credex — 
you can ignore it if it's not relevant.

**Q: How is this different from just reading pricing pages?**  
A: We cross-reference your specific stack for overlaps, seat mismatches, 
and plan mismatches — things that aren't obvious from reading one 
vendor's page at a time.
