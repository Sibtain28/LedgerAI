# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits at ~20-30% below retail. 
A startup spending $5,000/mo on AI tools is a $60k/yr customer. 
At 15% gross margin on credits, that's $9,000/yr gross profit per customer. 
Conservative LTV estimate (18-month retention): **$13,500**.

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Est. CAC | Basis |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Reddit/organic | $0 | Time cost only |
| Credex existing customer email | ~$50 | Internal email cost |
| Twitter thread (promoted) | ~$200 | Boost spend est. |
| Paid LinkedIn to CTOs | ~$800 | CPL at $40, 5% close |

## Conversion Funnel

- Landing page → audit started: **35%** (strong value prop, no friction)
- Audit started → completed: **60%** (short form, clear progress)
- Completed → email captured: **50%** (value shown before ask)
- Email captured → Credex consultation booked (high-savings only): **8%**
- Consultation → credit purchase: **25%**

**Blended**: 1,000 visitors → 350 start → 210 complete → 105 emails → 
~20 high-savings → 1.6 consultations → 0.4 purchases per 1,000 visitors.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

Assumptions:
- Average deal: $8,400/yr (startup spending $3,500/mo on AI credits)
- Need: 119 customers
- Conversion from audit to customer: 0.4 per 1,000 visitors
- Required visitors: ~300,000 over 18 months (~17k/mo)

17k/mo is achievable via: 3 viral HN/Reddit posts (50k total), 
SEO on "AI tool pricing" keywords (30k/mo at month 12), 
Credex customer base referrals (10k/mo), paid acquisition (5k/mo at $200 CAC).

**This works if**: The tool gets 2-3 organic viral moments in months 1-6 
that establish SEO and brand. The Credex existing customer email list 
is the seed that makes month 1 non-zero.
